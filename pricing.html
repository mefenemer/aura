Frequently Asked Questions

What are "raw computing costs"?
Think of our monthly subscription like paying for a car, and the "computing cost" like paying for the electricity. Most users spend less than £2 a month. We pass this cost to you with zero markup.
Is there a risk of a huge surprise bill?
No. You can set a strict monthly spending cap (e.g., £5). If your assistant reaches that, it simply pauses until you approve more.
What apps can my assistant connect to?
Almost anything (Gmail, Slack, Sheets, CRMs). Our team verifies compatibility during onboarding before you ever pay a dime.
Is my business data safe?
Your data is never used to train public AI models. It is locked in a secure vault that only belongs to your account.
Do I need to know anything about coding?
Absolutely not. You just fill out a plain-English form. Our human team takes over from there.
Am I locked into a long-term contract?
No. All plans are rolling monthly subscriptions. Cancel or change your assistant's job at any time.
