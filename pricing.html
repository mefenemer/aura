Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "task"?
Each time one of your assistants completes an action — drafts a reply, files a document, posts a report — that counts as one task. Browsing your dashboard or updating instructions does not count.
What happens when I reach my task limit?
You'll receive a notification when you reach 80% of your monthly allowance. At 100%, automated tasks are paused and you'll be prompted to upgrade or wait for the new billing period. Manual "on-command" tasks are never blocked.
Is there a risk of a surprise bill?
No. Every plan has a fixed flat fee — there are no per-task or per-compute charges. Your bill is exactly the same amount every month.
What apps can my assistant connect to?
Almost anything — Gmail, Slack, Google Sheets, HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, and hundreds more. Our team verifies compatibility during onboarding before you ever pay a penny.
Is my business data safe?
Your data is never used to train public AI models. It is locked in a secure vault that only belongs to your account. We are GDPR-compliant and happy to sign a data processing agreement.
Do I need to know anything about coding?
Absolutely not. You describe what you want in plain English on a short form. Our human team takes over from there.
Am I locked into a long-term contract?
No. All plans are rolling monthly subscriptions. Cancel or upgrade at any time — no notice period required.
