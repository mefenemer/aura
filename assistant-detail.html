Be More Swan Safe Content Benchmark

Active on every assistant — always

Every assistant you create is protected by the Be More Swan Safe Content Benchmark — a built-in safety layer that runs at the highest priority in every system prompt. It cannot be disabled, overridden, or bypassed by any workspace setting. This means your brand is always shielded from content that violates platform policies or community standards, no matter what.

No Sexually Explicit Content

Adult material, nudity, or suggestive content is blocked across all platforms.

No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Content that dehumanises individuals or groups based on protected characteristics is refused.

No Violence or Dangerous Content

Graphic violence, gore, and instructions for harmful acts are always blocked.

No Self-Harm Promotion

Content that promotes self-harm or endangers wellbeing is never produced.

No Illegal Acts or Facilitation

Content that facilitates fraud, trafficking, or other criminal activity is refused.

No Harassment or Targeted Abuse

Content designed to intimidate, dox, or coordinate attacks on individuals is blocked.

No Spam or Phishing

Deceptive, manipulative, or credential-harvesting content is never produced.

No Unauthorised Copyright Use

Reproducing protected material or private personal data without consent is refused.

No Identity-Based Bias or Stereotyping

Evaluations, tone, and recommendations remain strictly equitable and will never alter based on a subject's gender, ethnicity, religion, or sexuality.

Covers Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X — strictest platform standard always applies.
