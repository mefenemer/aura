Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Data Privacy

At Aura-Assist, we understand that you are trusting us with your business operations and data. We operate on a principle of strict data isolation: your business data belongs to you, and we do not use your information to train public AI models.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to build and manage your digital assistants, including:

  • Contact details provided during onboarding.
  • Workflow instructions and business process logic.
  • Data processed by your assistants (e.g., email content, lead form data) solely for the purpose of executing the tasks you have requested.

3. Data Isolation and Security

Your digital assistant's "memory" is stored in a secure, isolated vault specific to your account. We employ enterprise-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. We ensure that your proprietary information—such as your customer lists, pricing, or internal communications—remains confidential and is not shared with third-party model providers for training purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Build, test, and deploy your custom digital assistants.
  • Monitor assistant performance to ensure reliability.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates to your workflows or service notifications.

5. Your Control

You maintain full ownership of your workflows and your proprietary business data. You may request the deletion of your data or the cancellation of your assistant at any time.

Migration Policy:

Upon request, we will provide you with a plain-text export of your assistant's job instructions and workflow logic. Please note that while you own the logic, the specific technical architecture and integrations we have built are proprietary to Aura-Assist. We do not provide direct migration of technical infrastructure or "plug-and-play" files to third-party agencies. If you choose to switch providers, we will facilitate the handover by providing your documented instructions, and upon final confirmation, we will securely purge your account-specific assistant memory and associated data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our service or legal requirements. We will notify all active subscribers of significant updates.
