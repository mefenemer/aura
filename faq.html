Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Aura-Assist. Can't find the answer? Drop us a line.

Getting Started

Do I need to know anything about coding?
Absolutely not. You describe what you want in plain English on a short form. Our human team handles everything from there — building, connecting, and testing your assistant before it goes live.
How quickly can I get started?
You can sign up and complete your onboarding form in under 5 minutes. Once submitted, our team typically has your first assistant configured and ready within one business day.
What apps can my assistant connect to?
Almost anything — Gmail, WordPress, LinkedIn, Slack, Google Sheets, HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, and hundreds more. Our team verifies compatibility during onboarding before you ever pay a penny.

Billing & Plans

Is there a risk of a surprise bill?
No. Every plan is a fixed flat fee — there are no per-task or per-compute charges. Your bill is exactly the same amount every month.
Am I locked into a long-term contract?
No. All plans are rolling monthly subscriptions. Cancel or upgrade at any time — no notice period, no penalties.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll pro-rate the difference.

Tasks & Capacity

What counts as a completed task?
Each time your assistant completes an action — drafts a post, files a document, sends a report, or completes a workflow step — that counts as one task. Browsing your dashboard, scheduling, or updating instructions does not count.
What happens when I reach my task limit?
You'll receive an in-app notification at 80% of your monthly allowance. At 100%, automated tasks are paused and you'll be prompted to upgrade or wait for the next billing period. Manual on-command tasks are never blocked.
Do unused tasks roll over to the next month?
Task allowances reset each billing cycle and do not roll over. However, since the plans are flat-fee, you're always getting the same value regardless of how many tasks you use.

Safety & Privacy

What is the Brand Protection Guarantee?
Every assistant is governed by the Aura Safe Content Benchmark — a built-in set of safety guidelines that ensures your assistant never publishes anything harmful, off-brand, or legally problematic. This is included on every plan. Combined with Draft Approvals, you stay in full control at all times.
Is my data safe? Are you GDPR compliant?
Yes. All data is processed and stored in compliance with GDPR. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, and we never sell your data to third parties. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
Can my assistant publish content without my approval?
Only if you explicitly configure it that way. By default, every output enters a Draft Approvals queue so you can review and approve before anything goes live. You can choose to enable fully autonomous publishing for specific workflows once you're confident in your assistant's output quality.
